How to play

1. You will have three seconds to memorise where the locations of the images are.

2. Match two of the same images together to keep them face forward.

3. Try and match all the images to each other with the least amount of errors.

The challenge

Are you smarter than most of the population?

If you can match all cards with 0 errors then you are in for a chance to compete against the best in the world for a top prize!
